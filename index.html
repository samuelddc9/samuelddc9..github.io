About Team Tap Games

Team Tap Games is a dynamic game development studio dedicated to crafting cooperative experiences that bring players together, both through video games and board games. Established to merge traditional and digital gaming, Team Tap Games excels in creating engaging titles that emphasize couch co-op play, requiring teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking. Their flagship video game, "Monster Medbay," exemplifies this approach with its lively gameplay that encourages players to collaborate in treating fantastical creatures in a whimsical hospital setting.

The studio stands out for its commitment to quality and creativity across all types of gaming media. Each game, whether it's a board game or a video game, is meticulously designed with vibrant graphics, intuitive mechanics, and original storylines that appeal to a wide range of players. By focusing on couch co-op experiences, Team Tap Games fosters a social gaming environment where friends and families can enjoy rich, interactive play sessions together, right from their living rooms.
